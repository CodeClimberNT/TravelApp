# WanderShare - Travel Sharing App

Welcome to our Travel Sharing Application! This platform is designed to help users find and participate in experiences proposed and organized directly by other travelers—no agencies involved. 

The goal of this app is to connect travelers, facilitating the discovery, organization, and sharing of community-based travel experiences. Whether you are looking to join an exciting adventure or organize a trip to find travel buddies, this platform has you covered.

## Core Features

### 👤 User Profiles & Authentication
* **Seamless Onboarding**: User registration and secure login functionalities.
* **Personalized Profiles**: Manage your personal profile, including basic travel preferences (types of experiences sought, desired destinations) and a log of past travel experiences.

### ✈️ Discover & Propose Trips
* **Organize Experiences**: Create, edit, and delete travel proposals. Establish itineraries with specific stops, suggest activities (e.g., climbing, skiing, scuba diving, city exploration), and define group-time vs. free-time rules. Set price estimates and travel dates.
* **Import & Duplicate**: Easily recreate proposals by importing or duplicating existing ones published on the platform.
* **Explore & Filter**: Discover new adventures by filtering trips based on destination, price range, duration, travel group size, and trip type (e.g., adventure, culture, party, relax).

### 🤝 Apply & Manage Participants
* **Join Trips**: Apply to participate in a travel proposal, either individually or by requesting multiple spots (for unregistered companions).
* **Manage Applicants**: Trip organizers can easily review candidate profiles and seamlessly accept or reject applications.

### 🌟 Reviews & Community
* **Share Experiences**: Publish detailed reviews (including photos) and share valuable travel tips ("do's and don'ts") about your trips.
* **Travel Logs**: Automatically log your past trips taken via the platform.
* **Companion Feedback**: Review and rate your travel companions to build community trust.

### 🔔 Smart Notifications
Stay up-to-date with timely notifications regarding:
* Last-minute travel proposals.
* Recommended trips based on your preferences.
* New applications received for your proposals.
* Status updates on your pending applications.
* New reviews received from travel buddies.

## Getting Started

To run the application locally:
1. Clone this repository to your local machine.
2. Open the project in **Android Studio**.
3. Allow Gradle to sync dependencies.
4. Run the app on an Android Emulator or a connected physical device.
